<p>Given two lists Aand B, and B is an anagram of A. B is an anagram of A means B is made by randomizing the order of the elements in A.</p><p>We want to find an index mapping P, from A to B. A mapping P[i] = j means the ith element in A appears in B at index j.</p><p>These lists A and B may contain duplicates. If there are multiple answers, output any of them.</p><p>For example, given</p><p>A = [12, 28, 46, 32, 50]<br>B = [50, 12, 32, 46, 28]<br>We should return<br>[1, 4, 3, 2, 0]<br>as P[0] = 1 because the 0th element of A appears at B[1], and P[1] = 4 because the 1st element of A appears at B[4], and so on.<br>Note:</p><p>A, B have equal lengths in range [1, 100].<br>A[i], B[i] are integers in range [0, 10^5].</p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>题目大意：给两个数组A和B，B是A的同字母异序词，返回一个等长数组P，其中P[i] = j， 表示A的第i个元素在B的第j个元素处，如果有多个答案，返回一个即可～</strong></span></p><p><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">分析：设置一个二维数组v，将数字i对应的在B数组中的下标放在v[i]中，这样遍历A数组，每次取出一个v[A[i]]放入ans数组对应的i下标处即可～</span></strong></p><p> </p>
