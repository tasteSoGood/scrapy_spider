<p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Given N rational numbers in the form “numerator/denominator”, you are supposed to calculate their sum.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Input Specification:</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Each input file contains one test case. Each case starts with a positive integer N (&lt;=100), followed in the next line N rational numbers “a1/b1 a2/b2 …” where all the numerators and denominators are in the range of “long int”. If there is a negative number, then the sign must appear in front of the numerator.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Output Specification:</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>For each test case, output the sum in the simplest form “integer numerator/denominator” where “integer” is the integer part of the sum, “numerator” &lt; “denominator”, and the numerator and the denominator have no common factor. You must output only the fractional part if the integer part is 0.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Input 1:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>5</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>2/5 4/15 1/30 -2/60 8/3</strong></span><br><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Output 1:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>3 1/3</strong></span><br><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Input 2:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>2</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>4/3 2/3</strong></span><br><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Output 2:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>2</strong></span><br><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Input 3:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>3</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>1/3 -1/6 1/8</strong></span><br><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Output 3:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>7/24</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>题目大意：给N个有理数（以分子/分母的形式给出），计算这N个数的总和，最后总和要以（整数 分子/分母）的形式给出～</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>分析：先根据分数加法的公式累加，后分离出整数部分和分数部分</strong></span><br><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>分子和分母都在长整型内，所以不能用int存储，否则有一个测试点不通过</strong></span><br><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>一开始一直是浮点错误，按理来说应该是出现了/0或者%0的情况，找了半天也不知道错在哪里，</strong></span><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>后来注意到应该在累加的时候考虑是否会超出long long的范围，所以在累加每一步之前进行分子分母的约分处理，然后就AC了～</strong></span><br><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>应该还要考虑整数和小数部分都为0时候输出0的情况，但是测试用例中不涉及，所以如果没有最后两句也是可以AC的（PS：据说更新后的系统已经需要考虑全零的情况了～）</strong></span></p><p> </p>
