<p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>满射：</strong></span><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>对任意b，存在a满足f(a) = b。</strong></span><br><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1649" src="http://www.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/Snip20160613_129-300x286.png" alt="Snip20160613_129" width="300" height="286" srcset="https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/Snip20160613_129-300x286.png 300w, https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/Snip20160613_129.png 398w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px"><br><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">即：值域y是满的。每个y都有x对应。不存在某个y没有x对应的情况。</span></strong></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong><span style="color: #3366ff;">单射：</span>(one-to-one function) 一对一函数。x不同则y不同。</strong></span><br><img class="alignnone wp-image-1650 size-medium" src="http://www.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/Snip20160613_128-297x300.png" alt="Snip20160613_128" width="297" height="300" srcset="https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/Snip20160613_128-297x300.png 297w, https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/Snip20160613_128.png 392w" sizes="(max-width: 297px) 100vw, 297px"><br><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">即：没有一个x对应两个y，也没有一个y有对应两个x。</span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="color: #3366ff;">双射：</span><span style="color: #33cccc;">既是满射，也是单射。</span></strong><br><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1651" src="http://www.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/Snip20160613_130-293x300.png" alt="Snip20160613_130" width="293" height="300" srcset="https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/Snip20160613_130-293x300.png 293w, https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/Snip20160613_130.png 392w" sizes="(max-width: 293px) 100vw, 293px"><br><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">即：每个y都有x对应。而且都是一一对应。</span></strong></p>
