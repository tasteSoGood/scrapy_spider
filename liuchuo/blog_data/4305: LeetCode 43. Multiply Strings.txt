<p>Given two non-negative integers num1 and num2 represented as strings, return the product of num1 and num2.</p><p>Note:</p><p>The length of both num1 and num2 is &lt; 110.<br>Both num1 and num2 contains only digits 0-9.<br>Both num1 and num2 does not contain any leading zero.<br>You must not use any built-in BigInteger library or convert the inputs to integer directly.</p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>题目大意：给两个用字符串表示的非负整数，计算他们的乘积，结果用string表示～</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>分析：先将字符串num1和num2倒置，然后i和j分别遍历num1和num2，将num1[i] * num2[j]的结果加上v[i+j]本身的结果保存为temp，temp的余数保存在v[i+j]中，temp的进位累加在v[i+j+1]中。</strong></span><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>从后向前从第一个非0数字开始将数字转化为字符保存在result字符串中，result即为所求字符串～</strong></span></p><p> </p>
