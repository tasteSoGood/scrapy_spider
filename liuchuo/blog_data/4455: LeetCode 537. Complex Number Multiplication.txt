<p>Given two strings representing two complex numbers.</p><p>You need to return a string representing their multiplication. Note i2 = -1 according to the definition.</p><p>Example 1:<br>Input: “1+1i”, “1+1i”<br>Output: “0+2i”<br>Explanation: (1 + i) * (1 + i) = 1 + i2 + 2 * i = 2i, and you need convert it to the form of 0+2i.<br>Example 2:<br>Input: “1+-1i”, “1+-1i”<br>Output: “0+-2i”<br>Explanation: (1 – i) * (1 – i) = 1 + i2 – 2 * i = -2i, and you need convert it to the form of 0+-2i.<br>Note:</p><p>The input strings will not have extra blank.<br>The input strings will be given in the form of a+bi, where the integer a and b will both belong to the range of [-100, 100]. And the output should be also in this form.</p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>题目大意：给两个复数，求两个数的乘积～</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>分析：使用sscanf和ssprinf，(m+ni)*(p+qi)=(m*p-n*q)*(n*p+m*q) i</strong></span></p><p> </p>
