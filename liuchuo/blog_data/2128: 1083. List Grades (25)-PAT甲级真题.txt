<p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Given a list of N student records with name, ID and grade. You are supposed to sort the records with respect to the grade in non-increasing order, and output those student records of which the grades are in a given interval.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Input Specification:</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Each input file contains one test case. Each case is given in the following format:</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>N</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>name[1] ID[1] grade[1]</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>name[2] ID[2] grade[2]</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>… …</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>name[N] ID[N] grade[N]</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>grade1 grade2</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>where name[i] and ID[i] are strings of no more than 10 characters with no space, grade[i] is an integer in [0, 100], grade1 and grade2 are the boundaries of the grade’s interval. It is guaranteed that all the grades are distinct.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Output Specification:</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>For each test case you should output the student records of which the grades are in the given interval [grade1, grade2] and are in non-increasing order. Each student record occupies a line with the student’s name and ID, separated by one space. If there is no student’s grade in that interval, output “NONE” instead.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Input 1:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>4</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Tom CS000001 59</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Joe Math990112 89</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Mike CS991301 100</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Mary EE990830 95</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>60 100</strong></span><br><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Output 1:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Mike CS991301</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Mary EE990830</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Joe Math990112</strong></span><br><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Input 2:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>2</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Jean AA980920 60</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Ann CS01 80</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>90 95</strong></span><br><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Output 2:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>NONE</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>题目大意：给出n个考生的信息，按照分数从高到低排序，并且输出给定区间的考生信息。如果不存在满足条件的考生就输出NONE。</strong></span><br><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>分析：建立结构体数组，将不满足条件的学生grade改为-1，并统计满足条件的学生的个数cnt，然后进行排序，输出前cnt个考生的信息～～<br><span style="color: #ff99cc;">注意：因为每个学生的成绩都不同，所以按照下降排列即可，return a.grade &gt; b.grade;</span></strong></span></p><p> </p>
