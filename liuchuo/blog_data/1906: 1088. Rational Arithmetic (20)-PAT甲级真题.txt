The denominators are guaranteed to be non-zero numbers.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Output Specification:</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>For each test case, print in 4 lines the sum, difference, product and quotient of the two rational numbers, respectively.
