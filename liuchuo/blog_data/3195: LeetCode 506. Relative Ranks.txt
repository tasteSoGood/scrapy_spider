<p>Given scores of N athletes, find their relative ranks and the people with the top three highest scores, who will be awarded medals: “Gold Medal”, “Silver Medal” and “Bronze Medal”.</p><p>Example 1:<br>Input: [5, 4, 3, 2, 1]<br>Output: [“Gold Medal”, “Silver Medal”, “Bronze Medal”, “4”, “5”]<br>Explanation: The first three athletes got the top three highest scores, so they got “Gold Medal”, “Silver Medal” and “Bronze Medal”.<br>For the left two athletes, you just need to output their relative ranks according to their scores.<br>Note:<br>N is a positive integer and won’t exceed 10,000.<br>All the scores of athletes are guaranteed to be unique.</p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>题目大意：给一个数组，为几个运动员的分数，返回他们的排名，如果前三名应该为”Gold Medal”, “Silver Medal”, “Bronze Medal”，否则是数字名次，保证所有的分数不重复～</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>分析：拷贝一个相同的数组arr，然后排序，从nums[i]中寻找与arr[j]相同的分数，名次即为j+1。如果j为0，1，2则需要特殊处理～</strong></span></p><p> </p>
