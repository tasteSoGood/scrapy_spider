<p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>The basic task is simple: given N real numbers, you are supposed to calculate their average. But what makes it complicated is that some of the input numbers might not be legal. A “legal” input is a real number in [-1000, 1000] and is accurate up to no more than 2 decimal places. When you calculate the average, those illegal numbers must not be counted in.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Input Specification:</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Each input file contains one test case. For each case, the first line gives a positive integer N (&lt;=100). Then N numbers are given in the next line, separated by one space.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Output Specification:</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>For each illegal input number, print in a line “ERROR: X is not a legal number” where X is the input. Then finally print in a line the result: “The average of K numbers is Y” where K is the number of legal inputs and Y is their average, accurate to 2 decimal places. In case the average cannot be calculated, output “Undefined” instead of Y. In case K is only 1, output “The average of 1 number is Y” instead.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Input 1:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>7</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>5 -3.2 aaa 9999 2.3.4 7.123 2.35</strong></span><br><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Output 1:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>ERROR: aaa is not a legal number</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>ERROR: 9999 is not a legal number</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>ERROR: 2.3.4 is not a legal number</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>ERROR: 7.123 is not a legal number</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>The average of 3 numbers is 1.38</strong></span><br><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Input 2:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>2</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>aaa -9999</strong></span><br><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Output 2:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>ERROR: aaa is not a legal number</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>ERROR: -9999 is not a legal number</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>The average of 0 numbers is Undefined<br></strong></span><br><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>分析：用非常好用的sscanf和sprintf即可解决～<br>sscanf() – 从一个字符串中读进与指定格式相符的数据<br>sprintf() – 字符串格式化命令，主要功能是把格式化的数据写入某个字符串中</strong></span></p><p> </p>
