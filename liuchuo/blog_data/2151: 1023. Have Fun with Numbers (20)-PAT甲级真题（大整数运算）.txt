<p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Notice that the number 123456789 is a 9-digit number consisting exactly the numbers from 1 to 9, with no duplication. Check to see the result if we double it again!</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Now you are suppose to check if there are more numbers with this property.
