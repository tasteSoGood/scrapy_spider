It is hence assumed that all the houses are numbered from 1 to N, and all the candidate locations are numbered from G1 to GM.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Then K lines follow, each describes a road in the format</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>P1 P2 Dist</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>where P1 and P2 are the two ends of a road which can be either house numbers or gas station numbers, and Dist is the integer length of the road.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Output Specification:</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>For each test case, print in the first line the index number of the best location.
