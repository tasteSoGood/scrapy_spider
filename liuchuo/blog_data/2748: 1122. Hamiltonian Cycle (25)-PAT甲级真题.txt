<p><strong><span style="color: #33cccc;">The “Hamilton cycle problem” is to find a simple cycle that contains every vertex in a graph. Such a cycle is called a “Hamiltonian cycle”.</span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="color: #33cccc;">In this problem, you are supposed to tell if a given cycle is a Hamiltonian cycle.</span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="color: #3366ff;">Input Specification:</span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="color: #33cccc;">Each input file contains one test case. For each case, the first line contains 2 positive integers N (2&lt; N &lt;= 200), the number of vertices, and M, the number of edges in an undirected graph. Then M lines follow, each describes an edge in the format “Vertex1 Vertex2”, where the vertices are numbered from 1 to N. The next line gives a positive integer K which is the number of queries, followed by K lines of queries, each in the format:</span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="color: #33cccc;">n V<sub>1</sub> V<sub>2</sub> … V<sub>n</sub></span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="color: #33cccc;">where n is the number of vertices in the list, and V<sub>i</sub>‘s are the vertices on a path.</span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="color: #3366ff;">Output Specification:</span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="color: #33cccc;">For each query, print in a line “YES” if the path does form a Hamiltonian cycle, or “NO” if not.</span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="color: #3366ff;">Sample Input:</span></strong></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>6 10<br>6 2<br>3 4<br>1 5<br>2 5<br>3 1<br>4 1<br>1 6<br>6 3<br>1 2<br>4 5<br>6<br>7 5 1 4 3 6 2 5<br>6 5 1 4 3 6 2<br>9 6 2 1 6 3 4 5 2 6<br>4 1 2 5 1<br>7 6 1 3 4 5 2 6<br>7 6 1 2 5 4 3 1</strong> </span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><b>Sample Output:</b></span></p><p><strong><span style="color: #33cccc;">YES</span></strong><br><strong><span style="color: #33cccc;">NO</span></strong><br><strong><span style="color: #33cccc;">NO</span></strong><br><strong><span style="color: #33cccc;">NO</span></strong><br><strong><span style="color: #33cccc;">YES</span></strong><br><strong><span style="color: #33cccc;">NO</span></strong></p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>题目大意：给出一个图，判断给定的路径是不是哈密尔顿路径</strong></span></p><p><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">分析：1.设置falg1 判断节点是否多走、少走、或走成环</span></strong><br><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">2.设置flag2 判断这条路能不能走通</span></strong><br><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">3.当falg1、flag2都为1时是哈密尔顿路径，否则不是</span></strong></p><p></p>
