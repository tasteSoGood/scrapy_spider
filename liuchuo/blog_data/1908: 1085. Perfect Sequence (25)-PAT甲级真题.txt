<p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Given a sequence of positive integers and another positive integer p. The sequence is said to be a “perfect sequence” if M &lt;= m * p where M and m are the maximum and minimum numbers in the sequence, respectively.</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Now given a sequence and a parameter p, you are supposed to find from the sequence as many numbers as possible to form a perfect subsequence.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>Input Specification:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Each input file contains one test case. For each case, the first line contains two positive integers N and p, where N (&lt;= 105) is the number of integers in the sequence, and p (&lt;= 109) is the parameter. In the second line there are N positive integers, each is no greater than 109.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>Output Specification:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>For each test case, print in one line the maximum number of integers that can be chosen to form a perfect subsequence.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>Sample Input:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>10 8</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>2 3 20 4 5 1 6 7 8 9</strong></span><br><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>Sample Output:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>8<br></strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>题目大意：给定一个正整数数列，和正整数p，设这个数列中的最大值是M，最小值是m，如果M &lt;= m * p，则称这个数列是完美数列。现在给定参数p和一些正整数，请你从中选择尽可能多的数构成一个完美数列。输入第一行给出两个正整数N（输入正数的个数）和p（给定的参数），第二行给出N个正整数。在一行中输出最多可以选择多少个数可以用它们组成一个完美数列</strong></span></p><p><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">分析：简单题。首先将数列从小到大排序，设当前结果为result = 0，当前最长长度为temp = 0；从i = 0～n，j从i + result到n，【因为是为了找最大的result，所以下一次j只要从i的result个后面开始找就行了】每次计算temp若大于result则更新result，最后输出result的值～</span></strong></p><p><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">【solution 2】如果熟练使用upper_bound()，可以使用以下解法解决问题（代码由Github用户littlesevenmo提供，在此表达感谢）：</span></strong></p><p> </p>
