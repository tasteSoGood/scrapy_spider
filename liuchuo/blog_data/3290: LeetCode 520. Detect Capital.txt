<p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>520. Detect Capital</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong> Given a word, you need to judge whether the usage of capitals in it is right or not.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>We define the usage of capitals in a word to be right when one of the following cases holds:</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>All letters in this word are capitals, like “USA”.</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong> All letters in this word are not capitals, like “leetcode”.</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong> Only the first letter in this word is capital if it has more than one letter, like “Google”.</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong> Otherwise, we define that this word doesn’t use capitals in a right way.</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong> Example 1:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong> Input: “USA”</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong> Output: True</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong> Example 2:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong> Input: “FlaG”</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong> Output: False</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>题目大意：判断一个字母是否大小写正确：要么全是大写，要么全是小写，或者首字母大写其他小写，否则不满足题意～</strong></span><br><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong> 分析：判断word[0]和word[1]的大小写，如果word[0]是小写，那后面必须是小写，如果word[0]是大写word[1]是小写，那后面也必须是小写，如果word[0]是大写word[1]也是大写那么后面必须都是大写～</strong></span></p><p> </p>
