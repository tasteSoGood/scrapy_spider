<p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>414. Third Maximum Number</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Given a non-empty array of integers, return the third maximum number in this array. If it does not exist, return the maximum number. The time complexity must be in O(n).</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Example 1:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Input: [3, 2, 1]</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Output: 1</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Explanation: The third maximum is 1.</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Example 2:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Input: [1, 2]</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Output: 2</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Explanation: The third maximum does not exist, so the maximum (2) is returned instead.</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Example 3:</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Input: [2, 2, 3, 1]</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Output: 1</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Explanation: Note that the third maximum here means the third maximum distinct number.</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Both numbers with value 2 are both considered as second maximum.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>分析：因为set会自动排序，所以每次将数字放入set，如果set里面容量超过三个就把最小的那个移除～</strong></span><br><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>到最后如果set的大小不是3个，那就输出最大值，也就是*s.rbegin(), 如果set的大小是3个就输出*s.begin()即为第三大～</strong></span></p><p> </p>
