<p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>1012. The Best Rank (25)</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>To evaluate the performance of our first year CS majored students, we consider their grades of three courses only: C – C Programming Language, M – Mathematics (Calculus or Linear Algebra), and E – English. At the mean time, we encourage students by emphasizing on their best ranks — that is, among the four ranks with respect to the three courses and the average grade, we print the best rank for each student.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>For example, The grades of C, M, E and A – Average of 4 students are given as the following:</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>StudentID C M E A</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310101 98 85 88 90</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310102 70 95 88 84</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310103 82 87 94 88</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310104 91 91 91 91</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Then the best ranks for all the students are No.1 since the 1st one has done the best in C Programming Language, while the 2nd one in Mathematics, the 3rd one in English, and the last one in average.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Input</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Each input file contains one test case. Each case starts with a line containing 2 numbers N and M (&lt;=2000), which are the total number of students, and the number of students who would check their ranks, respectively. Then N lines follow, each contains a student ID which is a string of 6 digits, followed by the three integer grades (in the range of [0, 100]) of that student in the order of C, M and E. Then there are M lines, each containing a student ID.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Output</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>For each of the M students, print in one line the best rank for him/her, and the symbol of the corresponding rank, separated by a space.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>The priorities of the ranking methods are ordered as A &gt; C &gt; M &gt; E. Hence if there are two or more ways for a student to obtain the same best rank, output the one with the highest priority.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>If a student is not on the grading list, simply output “N/A”.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Input</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>5 6</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310101 98 85 88</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310102 70 95 88</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310103 82 87 94</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310104 91 91 91</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310105 85 90 90</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310101</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310102</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310103</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310104</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>310105</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>999999</strong></span><br><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Sample Output</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>1 C</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>1 M</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>1 E</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>1 A</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>3 A</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>N/A</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>题目大意：现已知n个考生的3门分数，平均分可以按照这三门算出来。然后分别对这四个分数从高到低排序，这样对每个考生来说有4个排名。k个查询，对于每一个学生id，输出当前id学生的最好的排名和它对应的分数，如果名次相同，按照A&gt;C&gt;M&gt;E的顺序输出。如果当前id不存在，输出N/A</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #ff99cc;"><strong>PS：感谢github用户<a style="color: #ff99cc;" href="https://github.com/fs19910227">@fs19910227</a>提供的pull request～</strong></span></p><p> </p>
