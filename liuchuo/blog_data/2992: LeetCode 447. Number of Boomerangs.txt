<p>Given n points in the plane that are all pairwise distinct, a “boomerang” is a tuple of points (i, j, k) such that the distance between i and j equals the distance between i and k (the order of the tuple matters).</p><p>Find the number of boomerangs. You may assume that n will be at most 500 and coordinates of points are all in the range [-10000, 10000] (inclusive).</p><p>Example:<br>Input:<br>[[0,0],[1,0],[2,0]]<br>Output:<br>2<br>Explanation:<br>The two boomerangs are [[1,0],[0,0],[2,0]] and [[1,0],[2,0],[0,0]]</p><p><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>分析：map(m[i] = j)用来存储点与点之间距离（其实是距离的平方，不影响结果）为i的个数j。对于某一点，距离它为i的个数若为j个，则能够构成的i，j，k这样的组数是j * (j – 1)个。每次累计得到的总结果即为所求～</strong></span></p><p> </p>
