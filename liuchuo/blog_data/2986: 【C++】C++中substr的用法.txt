<p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>substr有2种用法：</strong></span><br><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>假设：string s = “0123456789”;<br></strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>string sub1 = <span style="color: #99cc00;">s.substr(5);</span> //只有一个数字5表示从下标为5开始一直到结尾：sub1 = “56789”</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>string sub2 = <span style="color: #99cc00;">s.substr(5, 3);</span> //从下标为5开始截取长度为3位：sub2 = “567”</strong></span></p>
