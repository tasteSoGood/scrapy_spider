<p>使用<strong><span style="color: #00ccff;">Edit -&gt; Line -&gt; Reindent</span></strong>可以格式化代码。<strong>无需选中代码，它是对全文所有的内容进行格式化。</strong></p><p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-269" src="http://www.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/C8350EB3-57A7-40E1-B596-5CB0F12D07AA.jpg" alt="C8350EB3-57A7-40E1-B596-5CB0F12D07AA" width="1258" height="956" srcset="https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/C8350EB3-57A7-40E1-B596-5CB0F12D07AA.jpg 1258w, https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/C8350EB3-57A7-40E1-B596-5CB0F12D07AA-300x228.jpg 300w, https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/C8350EB3-57A7-40E1-B596-5CB0F12D07AA-768x584.jpg 768w, https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/C8350EB3-57A7-40E1-B596-5CB0F12D07AA-1024x778.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1258px) 100vw, 1258px"></p><p>Sublime里面是没有给Reindent设置快捷键的。可以自行设置。 <strong><span style="color: #00ccff;">Sublime Text 2 -&gt; Preferences -&gt;Key Bindings -Default</span></strong>(也有可能是User，具体打开来看看哪个里面是快捷键设置的代码。)</p><p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-267" src="http://www.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/60A8B734-E263-46E3-8A35-7A1DD873EFF0.jpg" alt="60A8B734-E263-46E3-8A35-7A1DD873EFF0" width="848" height="522" srcset="https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/60A8B734-E263-46E3-8A35-7A1DD873EFF0.jpg 848w, https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/60A8B734-E263-46E3-8A35-7A1DD873EFF0-300x185.jpg 300w, https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/60A8B734-E263-46E3-8A35-7A1DD873EFF0-768x473.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 848px) 100vw, 848px"></p><p>在第一行添加上你自己设置快捷键的代码，如： <strong><span style="color: #3366ff;">{ “keys”: [“ctrl+shift+r”], “command”: “reindent” , “args”:{“single_line”: false}},</span></strong> <span style="color: #ff99cc;">别忘记最后的那个逗号。。</span> 然后保存，关闭。 就可以用自己设置的快捷键来重排格式化代码了。</p><p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-268" src="http://www.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/B3B84D94-AE7F-44A6-8361-B1A48242A6AD.jpg" alt="B3B84D94-AE7F-44A6-8361-B1A48242A6AD" width="2052" height="616" srcset="https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/B3B84D94-AE7F-44A6-8361-B1A48242A6AD.jpg 2052w, https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/B3B84D94-AE7F-44A6-8361-B1A48242A6AD-300x90.jpg 300w, https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/B3B84D94-AE7F-44A6-8361-B1A48242A6AD-768x231.jpg 768w, https://cdn1.liuchuo.net/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/B3B84D94-AE7F-44A6-8361-B1A48242A6AD-1024x307.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 2052px) 100vw, 2052px"></p><p><span style="color: #ff99cc;">喵呜</span>，<span style="color: #3366ff;">喵呜</span>，<span style="color: #339966;">喵呜</span><span style="color: #cc99ff;">｡◕‿◕｡</span></p>
