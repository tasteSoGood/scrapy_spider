<p>Follow up for “Unique Paths”:</p><p>Now consider if some obstacles are added to the grids. How many unique paths would there be?</p><p>An obstacle and empty space is marked as 1 and 0 respectively in the grid.</p><p>For example,<br>There is one obstacle in the middle of a 3×3 grid as illustrated below.</p><p>[<br>[0,0,0],<br>[0,1,0],<br>[0,0,0]<br>]<br>The total number of unique paths is 2.</p><p>Note: m and n will be at most 100.</p><p><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">分析：这道题是上一道题的升级版~加了障碍物~加不加障碍物差别就在于，当当前地域有障碍物的时候，a[i][j] = 0，其余的不变：</span></strong><br><strong><span style="color: #ff9900;">0.a[0][0] = 1; 1.对于i==0的时候，为最上面一排，当前方格只能由左边方格来，所以a[i][j] = a[i][j-1];  2.对于j==0的时候，为最左边一排，当前方格只能由上边方格来，所以a[i][j] = a[i-1][j];  3.其余情况，当前方格能由左边和上边两个方向过来，所以a[i][j] = a[i-1][j] + a[i][j-1];  最后直到一直递推输出到终点(m-1, n-1)的时候return a[m-1][n-1]; </span></strong></p><p> </p>
