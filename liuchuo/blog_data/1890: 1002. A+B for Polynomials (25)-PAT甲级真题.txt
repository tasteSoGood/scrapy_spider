It is given that 1 &lt;= K &lt;= 10，0 &lt;= NK &lt; … &lt; N2 &lt; N1 &lt;=1000.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>Output</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>For each test case you should output the sum of A and B in one line, with the same format as the input.
