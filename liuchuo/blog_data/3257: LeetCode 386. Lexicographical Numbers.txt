<p><span style="color: #3366ff;"><strong>386. Lexicographical Numbers</strong></span><br><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Given an integer n, return 1 – n in lexicographical order.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>For example, given 13, return: [1,10,11,12,13,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9].</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #33cccc;"><strong>Please optimize your algorithm to use less time and space. The input size may be as large as 5,000,000.</strong></span></p><p><span style="color: #99cc00;"><strong>题目大意：给一个整数n，将1-n所有数按照字典序排序～</strong></span><br><span style="color: #ff9900;"><strong>分析：从1～9分别深度优先遍历，对于每一次遍历，继续深度优先它的10 * cur + i（i从1～9），只要符合范围的就将该数字cur放入result数组中～最后返回result即可～</strong></span></p><p> </p>
