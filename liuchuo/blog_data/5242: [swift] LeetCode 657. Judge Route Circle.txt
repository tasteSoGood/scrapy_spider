<p>Initially, there is a Robot at position (0, 0). Given a sequence of its moves, judge if this robot makes a circle, which means it moves back to the original place.</p><p>The move sequence is represented by a string. And each move is represent by a character. The valid robot moves are R (Right), L (Left), U (Up) and D (down). The output should be true or false representing whether the robot makes a circle.</p><p>Example 1:<br>Input: “UD”<br>Output: true<br>Example 2:<br>Input: “LL”<br>Output: false</p><p></p>
